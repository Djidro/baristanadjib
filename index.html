About Me

I've been crafting coffee since 2018, focusing on espresso perfection and customer satisfaction. My journey began in a small local café where I discovered my passion for the art of coffee making.

Over the years, I've honed my skills in specialty coffee preparation, latte art, and customer service. I love experimenting with new coffee blends and sharing my passion for coffee culture with others.

My goal is to continue growing in the specialty coffee industry, eventually opening my own café that serves as a community hub for coffee enthusiasts.
